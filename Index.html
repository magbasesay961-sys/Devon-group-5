About FarmAdvisor SL

Empowering Sierra Leone's Farming Communities.

Sierra Leone's economy depends heavily on agriculture, yet most farmers have limited access to expert advice, updated crop calendars, market price information, or weather alerts tailored to their district. FarmAdvisor SL bridges that gap.

Whether you are growing rice in Bombali, cassava in Kenema, or groundnuts in Bo, FarmAdvisor SL gives you the right information at the right time — completely free, directly on your phone.
